Myself

Hello, my name is Taylen, as you can tell by the header. I'm 24 years old and currently going for my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I've completed two years of Graphic Design at Oakland University and now I am about to complete my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (Fall 2022). I've worked in a handful of design organizations, designed for hundreds of clients, and after three years of that, I decided to switch my focus to something bigger.



Computer Science is what allows me to express ideas into code. From forums to bots, I've worked on quite a bit. Unfortunately, the hardest part is retaining all the coding knowledge throughout the years unless you work on it everyday.



My goals are far fetched and may never happen. From dreaming about the stars and what lies past where humans have gone, to brainstorming about products that could help combat pollution in the oceans. My biggest fear is that I will never accomplish tasks such as these, but with determination and a little hard work, anything is possible.
